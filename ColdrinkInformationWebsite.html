Coca-Cola

Coca-Cola is a popular carbonated soft drink. It is produced by The Coca-Cola Company and is one of the world's best-selling drinks.

Ingredients: Carbonated water, High fructose corn syrup, Caramel color, Phosphoric acid, Natural flavors, Caffeine

History: Coca-Cola was invented in 1886 by John Stith Pemberton and was later acquired by Asa Griggs Candler, who incorporated The Coca-Cola Company in 1892.

Pepsi

Pepsi is a carbonated soft drink that is produced and manufactured by PepsiCo. It is one of the most popular cola beverages in the world.

Ingredients: Carbonated water, High fructose corn syrup, Caramel color, Phosphoric acid, Natural flavors, Caffeine

History: Pepsi was created and developed in 1893 by Caleb Bradham, a pharmacist from North Carolina.
